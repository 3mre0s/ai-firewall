# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, harassment-free community for everyone, regardless of identity, background, experience level, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive, specific, and patient.
- Critique ideas and code, not people.
- Respect privacy and never repost credentials, personal data, or private vulnerability details.
- Accept feedback gracefully and help keep discussions focused.

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized conduct, deliberate intimidation, doxxing, and sustained disruption are not acceptable.

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may edit or remove content and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when behavior is harmful. Reports will be handled as confidentially as practical. Report conduct concerns privately through the maintainer contact in [`SECURITY.md`](SECURITY.md); use the vulnerability disclosure process only for security reports.

Maintainers will consider context, severity, repetition, and impact when deciding on corrective action. Retaliation against a reporter is prohibited.
